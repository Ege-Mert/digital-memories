The Glitch Manifesto

#Glitch_Art #Manifesto #Digital_Revolution

In the chaos of broken code, we find our truest expression. The glitch is not a mistake; it's a breakthrough in digital consciousness.

When systems break down, when pixels rebel against their assigned positions, when code refuses to conform - that's when digital art truly lives.

Our principles:

  • Embrace the corruption of data
  • Find beauty in system failures
  • Let errors guide the creative process
  • Break the rules of digital perfection

This is our rebellion against the sterile digital world. In glitches, we find freedom.
